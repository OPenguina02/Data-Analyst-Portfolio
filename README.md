# Branch Performance & Network Expansion Analytics

## Project Overview

This project simulates a lending analytics environment for a fintech company operating in the consumer finance and pawn lending industry.

The objective is to evaluate branch performance, compare lending products, and assess the effectiveness of network expansion strategies through an interactive Power BI dashboard supported by a fully synthetic relational dataset.

The project covers the complete analytics workflow, including business understanding, synthetic data generation, data modeling, KPI design, DAX development, and dashboard construction.

---

## Business Background

TrueMoney+ is a fictional fintech company specializing in consumer lending and pawn-based financing.

Founded in 2022, the company has pursued aggressive growth through branch network expansion across multiple provinces. As the network grows, management faces increasing challenges in determining:

* Which branches are performing most effectively
* Which locations have untapped growth potential
* Which products perform best in different markets
* Which branches should be prioritized for future expansion
* Where new policies and products should be piloted before wider rollout

Because branch expansion requires substantial investment in staffing, operations, facilities, and marketing, understanding branch-level performance has become a strategic priority.

---

## Business Problem

The company lacks a standardized framework for evaluating branch performance across the network.

Management needs a data-driven approach to answer questions such as:

* Which branches generate the strongest business results?
* Which branches demonstrate healthy growth while maintaining acceptable risk levels?
* What characteristics are commonly found among top-performing branches?
* Which products contribute most to revenue generation?
* Which products carry the highest credit risk?
* Which branches should be selected for future policy testing and product launches?

---

## Project Objectives

The project aims to:

1. Measure branch performance using lending, revenue, and risk metrics
2. Evaluate the effectiveness of the branch expansion strategy
3. Build a branch ranking and comparison framework
4. Assess product-level profitability and portfolio quality
5. Support future product rollout and policy deployment decisions

---

## Dataset

The entire dataset was generated programmatically using Python.

The data model simulates core business entities commonly found in lending organizations:

### Core Tables

* Customer
* Individual Customer
* Corporate Customer
* Loan
* Payment
* Product
* Branch
* Staff

### Supporting Tables

* Policy
* Policy Product
* Policy Branch
* Staff Branch History
* Calendar

The dataset contains simulated customer behavior, loan lifecycles, repayment patterns, branch assignments, policy campaigns, and product performance metrics.

## Disclaimer

This project is based on business processes and lending products commonly found in the consumer finance and pawn lending industry.

All data was synthetically generated for portfolio and educational purposes. No real customer, employee, branch, transaction, or company data was used in this project.

Any figures, patterns, rankings, or insights presented in the dashboard are derived entirely from simulated data and should not be interpreted as real-world business performance.
